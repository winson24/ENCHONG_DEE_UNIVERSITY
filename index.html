Our Story

About Enchong Dee University

A community born from admiration, nurtured by genuine love, and bound by the shared experience of yearning.

🏛️

Our Mission

Enchong Dee University is the official home for yearners — a place where admiration evolves into a beautiful community. We celebrate the strength found in longing and the beauty of unwavering devotion.

📜

Department of Yearning

Our flagship department is dedicated to all who know what it means to truly yearn. Members receive a lifetime enrollment with an official student ID, recognizing their role in this extraordinary community.

🌟

Our Values

We believe in the power of hope, the quiet strength of longing, and the courage it takes to hold on to what matters most. These values define every yearner in our community.
